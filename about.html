About us

At First Aid Guidance, we are committed to providing Guidelines to help individuals, to respond effectively in emergencies. Our goal is to guide you through the fundamentals of first aid, step-by-step instructions so you can act with confidence when emergencies arise. To create a world where first aid basic knowledge, and every person is empowered to step up and provide immediate care in any situation.

Our Guidelines

DO'S

Stay Calm & Assess the Situation Call Emergency Services (911 / 112 / 108)
Check for Responsiveness & Breathing

DO'S

Use Protective Equipment
Ensure Safety First
Keep the Person Comfortable

DONT'S

Don’t Panic
Don’t Move an Injured Person Unnecessarily
Don’t Touch Open Wounds with Bare Hands

DONT'S

Don’t Ignore Signs of Serious Injuries
Don’t Apply Home Remedies Without Knowledge
Don’t Leave the Person Alone
